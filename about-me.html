Sandeep Kumar – Managing Director and Founder

Sailli Viroha (Singapore and Malaysia)

Equipped with Master’s degree from UK in Physiotherapy. Before starting Sailli Viroha Pte Ltd in 2013, Sandeep was already in rehabilitation business for more than a decade. Sandeep had a dream to create most beautiful and equally comfortable shoes for consumers, With this dream, he started his search for a team of medical professionals, researchers, engineers and shoe manufacturers who could understand his idea, his dream and work to produce the “pieces of art” with “foot science” in it. Sandeep’s dream realised in his first product line- SOLACE. His passion and relentless pursuit for most beautiful and absolutely comfortable shoes continues to bring excitement and comfort for SOLACE consumers


The company also manufactures scientifically tested Solace Comfort Insoles for both formal and sports shoes.


Sandeep is experienced with successfully managing companies and business operations in different cultures and countries.

Pratiti Gautam Kaji – Director

Pratiti Gautam Kaji (Singapore and Malaysia)

A Senior Physiotherapist by profession, Pratiti provides support on operational issues of the company. Her ability to plan, organize and implement work processes provides Sailli Viroha a solid background to move forward.

Directors : Sandeep Sailli and Pratiti Gautam Kaji.
